*Dinner Dash is an intuitive online ecommerce platform for a restaurant to facilitate online ordering and enhance the customer experience. Users can browse menus, place orders, and track order status in real-time. Admins can manage items, categories, and orders efficiently.*

</div>

## _Key Features_ :

- _Robust Authentication and Role Management_
- _Menu Management with Categories_
- _Order Management and Status Tracking_
- _Email Notifications on Order Updates_
- _Pagination and Search for Items_

## _Tech Stack_ :

- _TailwindCSS For Styling_
- _Ruby On Rails For Development_
- _Hotwire Stimulus For Interactivity_
- _Devise & Pundit Gem For Auth_
- _PostgreSQL For Database_
